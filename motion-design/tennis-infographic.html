Animated Tennis Infographic

You only get one first time for everything. In this case this was my very first motion design video I created in After Effects. It bring a tennis infographic that I created to life through movement. This was part of our motion course at Algonquin where we were beginning to learn about keyframe animation, position, rotation, and scale within After Effects.
