Good to Talk App

The idea behind this app is to help people communicate in new areas around the world where they don’t speak the residential language.

The idea of this app is very simple. You would have the phone out on a table or something flat and stable. One user would speak in his or her language, and the other user would be able to read what they have said translated into their language on the app.

While the app’s idea is simple, it would be hard to produce in production because of voice recognition variables as well as implementing proper translation of what has been said. The idea for this ap has been experimented with within the design and interface, although it is still at the early stages of assembly.
