Illustrative Design

My style of illustrations provide a mix of flat graphics such as the poster for Company and the Senators Campaign. As well as other styles that are a bit more fluid using strokes and subtle gradients. I also enjoy coming with the initial design I will illustrate on paper and in one sample illustration I show how the final rendering was based off the rendered ink drawings.
